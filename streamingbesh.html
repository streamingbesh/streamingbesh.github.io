What is Streaming?

Streaming is a live or recorded video stream that can be played on any device
by the use of the internet without even downloading it.

Types of Streaming Content

There are many types of streaming content, it can be video, audio, or live streams.

  1. Video refers to the moving picture, accompanied by sound such as a picture in television.
  2. Audio requires the use of speech, music and sound effects. Unlike videos, it does
    not have any [moving] pictures to accommodate the sounds.
  3. Live Streaming is when the streamed video is sent over the Internet
    in real time, without first being recorded and stored.

Needs of Viewers

Viewers need good quality streaming when watching a stream in order to continue watching it. In addition
to this, a comfortable interaction with the streamers is what the viewers need.

Characteristics of a Good Stream

A stream is considered to be good when it has these following characteristics:

  • a high quality video
  • a video that is both interesting and entertaining
  • the streamer delivers exactly what the viewers requested in a stream/video
  • the streamer experiments with different things aside from the main content

    • Why do some streamers have less viewers?

    Some streamers have low-quality streaming and their streams are not entertaining, which is why viewers
    aren't really interested in them at all. Furthermore, some of the streamers are new to streaming and their
    contents are not quite unique yet .
